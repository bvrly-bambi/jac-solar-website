Why Solar

Four reasons the Philippines is going solar — fast

Electricity rates keep climbing. Solar isn't just an alternative — for millions of Filipino homes and businesses, it's become the smarter financial decision.

Clean & Renewable Energy

The sun never runs out. Solar energy can be harnessed anywhere in the Philippines — rain or shine, on rooftops or open fields — at zero fuel cost.

Cuts Your Monthly Electricity Bill

A well-designed solar system can cover the majority of your energy needs — which means significantly less to pay Meralco or your local utility every month.

Low Maintenance, Long-Term Value

Solar systems require minimal upkeep — typically just cleaning a few times a year. Most quality panels carry warranties of 20 to 25 years.

Better for the Environment

Every kilowatt of solar means less dependence on fossil fuels. Going solar is one of the most practical ways to reduce your household or business carbon footprint.
