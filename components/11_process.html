How It Works

Your solar journey in four clear steps

No complicated process — a clear, transparent path from first conversation to a fully activated system delivering savings.

Step 01

Consultation & Analysis

We begin by reviewing your electricity bill and evaluating your current energy consumption. Whether it's a digital review or an initial site visit, we work to identify the most efficient solar solution for your unique property — not necessarily the biggest or most expensive one.

Step 02

Engineering & Design

Our engineers design a tailor-fit solar system based on your actual load data, roof structure, and budget — not a generic template.

Step 03

Installation

Our certified team installs your system using only Tier-1 components — cleanly, safely, and with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Step 04

Activation & Support

We commission and activate your system, hand over complete documentation, and provide ongoing after-sales engineering support for the life of your system.
